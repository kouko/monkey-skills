# Voice Quadrant Positioning

Tier 3 standard: fully self-contained. Defines the 2-axis voice
quadrant (**Authority↔Affinity × Reason↔Emotion**) as a **macro
typology** for initial voice-family selection. Complements the
**4-axis micro-tuning model** in `voice-and-tone.md` (Formality /
Seriousness / Respectfulness / Enthusiasm). The two tools are
sequential: quadrant first (strategic positioning), 4-axis second
(tactical fine-tuning).

## The Framework

### The 2 axes

```
            Authority (高社會距離 / institutional)
                  ↑
    Q1 知識權威型   |   Q2 意識形態型
    (Authority +   |   (Authority +
     Reason)       |    Emotion)
                   |
Reason ────────────┼──────────────── Emotion
                   |
    Q4 直覺行動型   |   Q3 情緒共鳴型
    (Affinity +    |   (Affinity +
     Reason)       |    Emotion)
                  ↓
            Affinity (低社會距離 / peer)
```

**Vertical axis: Authority ↔ Affinity** (grounded in Halliday Tenor)
- Authority = high social distance, institutional legitimacy,
  formality, expertise signaling
- Affinity = low social distance, peer register, warmth, solidarity

**Horizontal axis: Reason ↔ Emotion** (grounded in Vaughn FCB Think/Feel)
- Reason = logical/evidential register, data-driven, specification-
  focused
- Emotion = affective register, narrative, identity-driven

### Relationship to the 4-axis model

The quadrant and the 4-axis models are **complementary**, not
competing:

| Macro (Quadrant) | ≈ Compression of | Micro (4-axis) |
|------------------|------------------|----------------|
| Authority ↔ Affinity | ≈ | Formality + Respectfulness |
| Reason ↔ Emotion | ≈ | Seriousness + Enthusiasm |

**Workflow**: Quadrant first (strategic — which voice family?),
4-axis second (tactical — how formal within that family?).

## The Four Quadrants

### Q1: 知識權威型 (Authority + Reason)

**Definition**: Logical, structured, data-driven. Establishes
authority via evidence, precision, and institutional legitimacy.

**Linguistic markers**:
- Declarative sentences; subject-verb-object parallelism
- Technical/specialist vocabulary with precise definition
- Data and citation as primary evidence
- Low 虛詞 density (minimal 吧/喔/呢/囉); no emoji
- Long cohesive paragraphs

**Use cases**: B2B, financial services, medical, technical
documentation, deep-analysis journalism.

**Representative practitioners**:

| Language | Practitioner | Signature |
|----------|--------------|-----------|
| EN | David Ogilvy | Fact-based, "respect the reader" |
| EN | Rory Sutherland (Ogilvy) | Behavioral-economics logic |
| ZH | 葉明桂 (Ogilvy Taiwan CSO) | Insight-led strategy, "偏心" framing |
| ZH | 林育聖 (文案的美) | Method-over-talent pragmatism |
| JP | 佐藤尚之 | Fan-base theory, structured communication |
| JP | 古川裕也 (Dentsu) | Strategic advertising logic |

**Sample sentence patterns** (Q1 anchors):
- 葉明桂: "品牌不是定位出來的，是長出來的。" (insight-led assertion)
- 林育聖: "方法比才華更重要。" (pragmatic declarative)
- Ogilvy: "The consumer isn't a moron; she is your wife."

**Representative brands**:
- EN: The Economist, McKinsey
- ZH: 報導者 The Reporter, 玉山銀行 E.SUN Bank
- JP: 日経新聞, SONY

### Q2: 意識形態型 (Authority + Emotion)

**Definition**: Value-driven, aesthetic, identity-constructing.
Establishes authority via worldview and cultural-capital signaling.

**Linguistic markers**:
- Abstract nouns, conceptual vocabulary
- Aphoristic sentence structure
- Literary/philosophical register
- Low 虛詞 density; punctuation controls rhythm
- Manifesto-style assertion

**Use cases**: Premium/luxury, bookstores, lifestyle brands,
cultural institutions, manifesto-driven campaigns.

**Representative practitioners**:

| Language | Practitioner | Signature |
|----------|--------------|-----------|
| EN | Lee Clow (TBWA) | Apple "Think Different" |
| EN | Dan Wieden (W+K) | Nike "Just Do It" |
| ZH | 許舜英 (意識形態廣告) | Anti-utilitarian, aphoristic |
| ZH | 李欣頻 (誠品) | Literary, meditative |
| JP | 仲畑貴志 | Minimalist philosophical depth |
| JP | 大貫卓也 | Absolute aesthetic assertion |

**Sample sentence patterns** (Q2 anchors):
- 許舜英: "女性主義就是失敗在愛情和衣服這兩件事情上。"
- 李欣頻: 誠品 "我在閱讀中，遇見另一個自己。"
- Wieden: "Just Do It."

**Representative brands**:
- EN: Apple (Think Different era), Patagonia
- ZH: 誠品書店 (意識形態 era 1990s-2000s), Aesop (TW positioning)
- JP: 無印良品 MUJI, 蔦屋書店 TSUTAYA

### Q3: 情緒共鳴型 (Affinity + Emotion)

**Definition**: Warm, conversational, empathetic. Establishes
connection via "我也一樣" peer-register and everyday observation.

**Linguistic markers**:
- Conversational fragments, ellipsis
- Everyday-life vocabulary, observational specificity
- Medium-to-high 虛詞 density (呢/吧/喔 permitted)
- Second-person address, inclusive pronouns ("我們", "you")
- Rhythmic use of space and line breaks

**Use cases**: Everyday retail, family/household brands, social
campaigns, Affinity stage of PASONA long-form.

**Representative practitioners**:

| Language | Practitioner | Signature |
|----------|--------------|-----------|
| EN | Seth Godin | Tribal leadership, human-scale |
| EN | Leo Burnett | Plain-folk warmth |
| ZH | 龔大中 (Ogilvy Taiwan CCO) | 經濟美學 observational wit |
| ZH | 盧建彰 | Emotional realism, civic humanism |
| JP | **糸井重里** | State proposal, intentional ambiguity (see `jp-copy-craft-lineage.md`) |
| JP | **岩崎俊一** | Seasonal, impermanence (see `jp-copy-craft-lineage.md`) |

**Sample sentence patterns** (Q3 anchors):
- 龔大中 (全聯): "長得漂亮是本錢，把錢花得漂亮是本事。"
- 龔大中 (全聯): "知道一生要去的20個地方之後，我決定先去全聯。"
- 糸井重里: "おいしい生活。"
- 岩崎俊一: "恋を何年、休んでますか。"

**Representative brands**:
- EN: Dove (Real Beauty), Airbnb (Belong Anywhere)
- ZH: 全聯福利中心 (經濟美學), IKEA Taiwan (30-year 人生故事)
- JP: JR九州 (旅情), ほぼ日刊イトイ新聞

### Q4: 直覺行動型 (Affinity + Reason)

**Definition**: Efficient, action-oriented, solution-focused. Peer-
register directness combined with rational benefit clarity.

**Linguistic markers**:
- Short imperative sentences
- Numerical specifics (price, time, quantity)
- Low abstract-noun ratio
- Minimal 虛詞 (too many weakens urgency)
- Action verbs in leading position

**Use cases**: E-commerce, app onboarding, promotional push, micro-
conversion (opt-in/subscribe/download — see
`light-action-frameworks.md` PREP/CREMA).

**Representative practitioners**:

| Language | Practitioner | Signature |
|----------|--------------|-----------|
| EN | Gary Vaynerchuk | Action-first, hustle pragmatism |
| EN | Dave Trott | Simple, direct, problem-solving |
| ZH | 織田紀香 (諾利嘉) | Conversion-focused, SME-oriented |
| ZH | 廣告樂血研究院 (Wawa) | Performance-marketing education |
| JP | 佐藤可士和 | Ultra-simple signaling (超整理術) |
| JP | 岸勇希 (ex-Dentsu) | Behavior-design communication |

**Sample sentence patterns** (Q4 anchors):
- Shopee: "今天下單，明天脫單。" (Valentine's 2020)
- Shopee: "月初囤棉最便宜。" (2021 金句獎)
- Vaynerchuk (conceptual): "You don't need more time, you need more
  decisions."

**Representative brands**:
- EN: Amazon, Netflix
- ZH: 蝦皮購物 Shopee
- JP: UNIQLO, メルカリ Mercari

## Quadrant Mixing Rules

### Natural adjacencies

Adjacent quadrants combine relatively safely when shifts are
deliberate:

- **Q2 ↔ Q3** (Emotion shared): identity ↔ empathy bridge.
  Precedent: high-end lifestyle brands that layer manifesto (Q2) on
  emotional-resonance ads (Q3) — e.g., Patagonia campaigns.
- **Q1 ↔ Q4** (Reason shared): authority ↔ efficiency bridge.
  Precedent: B2B SaaS that uses analyst-report language in hero
  copy (Q1) and action-oriented CTAs (Q4).
- **Q1 ↔ Q2** (Authority shared): evidence ↔ manifesto bridge.
- **Q3 ↔ Q4** (Affinity shared): empathy ↔ action bridge. Precedent:
  long-form PASONA Affinity→Action flow.

### Collapse-risk pairings

Diagonal pairings in the same piece produce voice collapse:

- **Q1 + Q3** (Authority-Reason + Affinity-Emotion): analyst register
  next to empathy register reads as tonally schizophrenic.
- **Q2 + Q4** (Authority-Emotion + Affinity-Reason): manifesto
  aesthetic next to transactional CTA reads as brand trying-too-hard.

**Rule**: stay within one quadrant per piece; if crossing, traverse
via adjacency (Q2→Q3 or Q1→Q4), not diagonal.

## ZH Copywriting Tradition

### Why this section exists

The copywriting-team has deep JP voice grounding (糸井 / 岩崎 / 眞木 /
谷山 in `jp-copy-craft-lineage.md`) and EN Ogilvy tradition (in
`voice-and-tone.md`). ZH (Traditional Chinese / Taiwanese) voice
tradition has been under-represented. This section fills that gap.

### ZH tradition summary

Taiwan advertising has four distinguishable voice families that
map cleanly onto the quadrant:

| Quadrant | Taiwan lineage | Era |
|----------|---------------|-----|
| Q1 | 奧美策略派 (Ogilvy TW strategist tradition) — 葉明桂 | 1984– |
| Q2 | 意識形態廣告 — 許舜英 / 李欣頻 / 誠品 | 1990s–2000s |
| Q3 | 全聯經濟美學派 — 龔大中 / 盧建彰 | 2015– |
| Q4 | 數位行銷實務派 — 織田紀香 / 廣告樂血研究院 | 2010s– |

### ZH micro-adjustment indicators (HEURISTICS, NOT CANONICAL METRICS)

**Important**: these 3 indicators are **practical heuristics proposed
for this skill**, not academically published advertising-linguistics
metrics (see drift #28). Use for quadrant-alignment diagnosis only.

1. **虛詞使用頻率 (particle density)**
   - Low (Q1, Q2, Q4): ≤2 particles per 100 chars. Precision + brevity.
   - High (Q3): 3-6 particles per 100 chars. Conversational warmth.
   - Example contrast:
     - Q1: "本產品具備四項核心功能。"
     - Q3: "這款東西功能還滿多的喔，有四個很實用呢。"

2. **外來語混合比 (loanword mix)**
   - **日系** (職人/こだわり/奢華): Q2 luxury / craftsmanship voice
   - **英文化** (Focus / Value / Optimization): Q1 B2B voice
   - **在地** (CP值 / 接地氣 / 小確幸 / 奧客): Q3/Q4 conversational
     voice
   - Cross-mixing (Q1 using 在地 slang) typically collapses voice.

3. **標點節奏 (punctuation pacing)**
   - Prose rhythm (dense 。，): Q1/Q2 long-form register
   - Respiratory rhythm (spaces replacing punctuation): Q2/Q3
     literary/reflective register (e.g., 誠品-style)
   - Exclamatory rhythm (！heavy, emoji): Q4 action-urgency

### When to apply

- At **intake** (`copywriting-brainstorming.md` Q6): optional
  quadrant declaration with target practitioner reference
- At **drafting** (write-long-form / mid-form / short-form / light-
  action): use the sample sentence patterns as stylistic anchors
- At **gate** (`voice-consistency-gate.md` Dim 5): mechanical
  distinguishability via these 3 indicators

### With `voice-and-tone.md` (Tier 2)

- Quadrant is **macro** (voice family selection)
- 4-axis is **micro** (positioning within family)
- Use quadrant first, 4-axis second
- The 4-axis model remains the operational vocabulary for Brand
  Voice Guide authoring; quadrant is a strategic prelude

### With `jp-copy-craft-lineage.md` (Tier 3)

- 糸井 / 岩崎 live in **Q3** (Affinity + Emotion)
- 眞木 sits at **Q2 ↔ Q3 border** (Ideology-Emotion leaning)
- Quadrant provides a typology frame; JP lineage standard provides
  voice-master deep-dive

### With `long-form-pasona-canon.md`

- **Affinity stage** naturally Q3-leaning
- **Problem stage** Q1/Q3 mix (diagnosis + empathy)
- **Offer/Action stages** naturally Q4-leaning
- Crossing Q3→Q4 inside one long-form is safe (Affinity shared);
  crossing Q2→Q4 is collapse-risk

### With `persuasion-psychology-anchor.md` Schwartz Levels

**Schwartz × Quadrant interaction** (critical routing rule):

| Schwartz Level | Preferred Quadrant | Avoid |
|----------------|-------------------|-------|
| Level 1 (Most Aware) | Q4 (direct action) | Q2 (over-concept) |
| Level 2 (Product Aware) | Q4 / Q1 | — |
| Level 3 (Solution Aware) | Q1 / Q3 | — |
| Level 4 (Problem Aware) | Q3 (empathy entry) | Q4 direct push |
| **Level 5 (Unaware)** | **Q3 narrative entry** | **Q4 collapses conversion** |

**Rule**: Never use Q4 (direct action) for Level 5 Unaware readers —
mirrors the Schwartz 1966 no-direct-Offer rule.

## Anti-Patterns

- **Using the quadrant as a hard label rather than a starting
  position**. Voice lives on a continuum; quadrant is orientation,
  not prison. A brand can legitimately sit on a Q2↔Q3 edge.
- **Diagonal quadrant mixing in one piece** (Q1+Q3 or Q2+Q4) without
  deliberate transition craft. Produces voice collapse (tonal
  schizophrenia).
- **Schwartz × Quadrant mismatch**: using Q4 direct-action voice for
  Schwartz Level 5 Unaware readers. Collapses conversion; mirrors
  the Schwartz no-direct-Offer rule.
- **Citing brand archetypes as empirical taxonomy**. Archetypes are
  practitioner heuristic (contested framework per Neher 1996 /
  Xara-Brasil 2018 / Dias & Dias 2022). Use as voice-ideation prompt
  only; do not build gate criteria on archetype conformance.
- **Treating ZH micro-indicators as published linguistics**. 虛詞
  density / loanword mix / punctuation pacing are team heuristics
  (drift #28), not academically published advertising-linguistics
  metrics. Cite as operational tools, not canonical taxonomy.
- **Citing Vaughn or Halliday as the source of the combined quadrant**.
  Vaughn's Think/Feel and Halliday's Tenor are the axis groundings;
  the 2-axis combination is team synthesis (drift #25).
- **Using 詹宏志 as a Q1 copywriting exemplar**. He is editor /
  publisher / essayist — his voice is quadrant-relevant but scope-
  mismatched for ad copywriting (drift #27).
- **Referring to 廣告樂血幫**. The correct name is 廣告樂血研究院
  (drift #26).
- **Forcing the quadrant onto briefs that don't need positioning
  declaration**. For briefs with no quadrant declaration, the gate
  dimension is NOT_APPLICABLE; do not invent a position to evaluate.
