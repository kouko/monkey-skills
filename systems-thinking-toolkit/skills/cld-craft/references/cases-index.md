case section is sufficient. For high-stakes diagrams (legal / financial /
public-policy), regulatory artifacts, or first-time use of the
split-trick or variance audit, read `cases.md` end-to-end — the
interactions between cases are themselves load-bearing (e.g. Case 4
split-trick + Case 9 variance audit must both be applied when both
patterns appear together).
