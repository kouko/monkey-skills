## ARM A — SINGLE-PASS SYNTHESIS (the artifact under audit)
SUMMARY: ASML holds an uncontested monopoly on EUV lithography with a €39B backlog and €27.6B in 2023 revenue — structural moat arguments are sound. However, the bull case carries meaningful near-term headwinds: China export restrictions threaten ~29% of system revenue, order intake collapsed 61% quarter-over-quarter in Q3 2023 signaling cyclical softness, and the stock trades at ~35x forward P/E — a monopoly premium that prices in a recovery that remains uncertain in timing. Doubling down is defensible as a long-duration bet on AI-driven semiconductor capex, but the risk/reward depends critically on your existing position size, cost basis, time horizon, and whether China revenue erosion is already reflected in your entry price.
