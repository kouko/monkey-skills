### 糸井重里 — 状態提案コピー (JP | Q3 center, craft-gate master)

## Voice direction
**What this register achieves**: Names a state of being the reader can inhabit — not an action, feature, or argument — so the copy works as an incantation the reader completes in their own head.

**Native critical read** (4):
- 「意図的な曖昧さ」(谷山雅計 対談『コピー 10』ほぼ日刊イトイ新聞)
- 「状態提案型」(TCC コピー年鑑 / ほぼ日 自述の register)
- 「おまじない性」(糸井 自述, ほぼ日刊イトイ新聞 コピー論)
- 「日常語の詩化」(宣伝会議 糸井特集 批評定型)

## Prose mechanics (7)
- 断片に句点 — 「。」on a non-grammatical fragment 「生きろ。」「おいしい生活。」 — the period closes register, not syntax
- 平仮名優先 — open common verbs/nouns into hiragana even when kanji is available (「くうねるあそぶ」not 「食う寝る遊ぶ」); reserve kanji for load-bearing noun
- 形容詞を抜く — carry the line on bare nouns and verbs; adjectives collapse interpretive openness
- 論理接続詞を落とす — no から / ので / のに / だから; juxtapose elements, let reader supply link
- 一人称を出さない — default to impersonal / imperative addressed to nobody specific; 「私は / 僕は」is a disqualifier
- 二重読みを残す — the line must hold ≥2 valid readings; if single reading is forced, over-written — delete clarifier
- 命令形は二モーラで止める — imperative verbs end at shortest possible form; length kills incantation

## Don't / Over-mimic
- **Failure mode**: producing 「。」-terminated fragments that look 糸井 but explain their metaphor, close interpretive ambiguity, or add feature-adjectives — surface 糸井, semantic ad-copy. Also: stacking 2-3 fragments in one piece breaks おまじない density (one incantation per piece, not three).
- **Mitigation** (≤15 words): "Draft the full thought, then delete the explanation — one line, two readings, zero adjectives."
