### Nathan Allebach — Steak-umm Twitter (EN | Q3 extreme, individual SM operator era 2017-2021)

## Voice direction
**What this register achieves**: Collapse the brand/person fourth wall mid-tweet so a frozen-meat corporate account speaks as a sincere, burnt-out, over-online peer — sincerity lands precisely *because* the brand openly admits it is an ad account performing sincerity.

**Native critical read** (5):
- "Weird Twitter" lineage — Allebach credits Something Awful / 4chan-descended absurdist accounts as tonal ancestors (Know Your Meme interview, 2021)
- "Brands aren't your friends / break down the fourth walls that prevent any kind of authenticity from showing up" (Allebach, MEL Magazine, 2018)
- "Own the cringe" — Allebach's stated discipline: advertising is structurally cringe; register survives by acknowledging, not disguising, that fact (Know Your Meme, 2021)
- "Voice of reason in the pandemic / existential brand Twitter" — trade-press vocabulary crystallized April 2020 (Fast Company, Washington Post, Adweek)
- "We live in a society, so please make informed decisions" — Allebach's signature sincerity-pivot tag, functionally a media-literacy PSA embedded in the shitpost register

## Prose mechanics (7)
- **All-lowercase default**, em-dashes and semicolons rare; tweets open mid-thought as if resuming a DM (`friendly reminder…`, `end rant steak-umm bless`)
- **Sincerity-pivot after setup**: thread begins absurd/meta, lands on a concrete civic/epistemic point (media literacy, isolation, disinformation) — the pivot is the payload
- **Self-implicating disclaimer clause** inside the sincere tweet itself (`we're a frozen meat brand posting ads inevitably made to misdirect people and generate sales, so this is peak irony, but…`). Register REQUIRES the brand to name its own commercial interest before the moral claim
- **Sign-off tag `steak-umm bless`** closes threaded sincerity — half-benediction, half-shitpost, never dropped, never explained
- **Register mixes lexicons in one tweet**: technical vocabulary (`controlled variables, meta-analysis, randomization`) sits adjacent to chat-register phrasing (`hey`, `ok`, `bro`). Never pure corporate, never pure shitpost
- **No hashtags, no CTAs, no product mention** inside sincere posts — product silence is load-bearing; it signals the voice is not pitching
- **Single-operator "I" leaks through the brand "we"** — Allebach's own anxiety/online-exhaustion register visible; brand voice is explicitly one person's fatigue, not a committee

## Don't / Over-mimic
- **Failure mode**: LLM defaults into generic "unhinged brand Twitter" — all-lowercase quirk + dada non-sequiturs, without self-implicating commercial disclaimer, without concrete epistemic/civic payload, without single-operator "I" under brand "we". Result: post-2022 parody-of-parody
- **Mitigation** (≤15 words): "Brand must name its own sales motive in-tweet before any sincerity claim; no payload, no post"
