## Detailed entries

### JP Q1 center — 朝日新聞「天声人語」

- **Era**: 1904-01-05 → present (122 years). Rebadged 1940, restored 1945.
- **Source**: 朝日新聞 論説委員 rotation, anonymous under nameplate「天声人語子」
- **Primary sources**:
  - [天声人語 Wikipedia JA](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/天声人語)
  - [Tensei Jingo Wikipedia EN](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tensei_Jingo)
  - 原書房『英文対照 天声人語』Vol.220 Spring 2025 (ISBN series)
- **Voice signature**:
  - 607-char fixed length (forces compression discipline)
  - 季語/俳句/古典引用 → 時事 pivot → 短 close
  - Quiet authority; observational not first-person emotional
  - Model: early-20th-century English essay tradition
- **Cross-ref to zh-TW**: STRONG — 聯合報「黑白集」/ 中國時報「短評」 direct structural parallel
- **Over-mimic mitigation**: "sample only tonal register, not the seasonal-hook move"

### JP Q1 extreme — ロイター日本語版

- **Era**: Reuters founded 1851; Japan-language service long-running
- **Primary sources**:
  - [Reuters Handbook of Journalism](https://jp.reuters.com/)
  - [urayomi-news 2025 ロイター vs ブルームバーグ 文体比較](https://urayomi-news.com/2025/11/03/media-reading-vol2-reuters-bloomberg/)
- **Voice signature**:
  - 評価語を極力削る (zero evaluative language)
  - 数字を先頭付近
  - Source-tagging confidence stratification
  - 判断を読者に委ねる設計
- **Cross-ref to zh-TW**: STRONG — 中央社 wire copy parallel
- **Note**: This is international wire with JP localization, not pure JP-native authority. If purely JP-native required, fall back to 日経新聞 社説 (but it carries residual 起承転結 warmth disqualifying it from *true* extreme)
- **Skip-caution**: 白書 (government register) flagged HIGH over-mimic — use as reference only, NOT active anchor

### JP Q4 center — クックパッド step voice

- **Era**: 1998 → present; ~3.96M recipes
- **Source**: User-generated + Cookpad 編集部 guidelines
- **Primary sources**:

  - [材料表記ガイド](https://cookpad.com/channels/5/articles/215)

  - 「つくれぽ」feedback-loop rhythm
- **Cross-ref to zh-TW**: STRONG — 愛料理 / iCook recipe voice

### JP Q4 extreme — ジャパネットたかた 高田明 (1990-2015 founder era ONLY)

- **Critical boundary**: ONLY 高田明 (founder) era; NOT 高田旭人 (son succession post-2015)
- **Primary sources**:
  - [高田明 Wikipedia](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/高田明)
  - [日経ビジネス「2秒の間」](https://business.nikkei.com/atcl/opinion/16nv/032200016/032200001/)
  - ISBN 978-4-492-04590-2 『伝えることから始めよう』高田明, 東洋経済新報社
- **Verbatim signature lines**:
  - 「この商品はなんと 2万9800円。分割金利手数料はジャパネットが負担します」
  - 「金利・手数料は負担」
  - 「伝えたつもりになるな、本当に伝わったか検証しなさい」(高田自己引用)
- **Voice signature**:
  - 「序破急」構成 (世阿弥 引用)
  - 「伝えた vs 伝わった」craft distinction
  - 「2秒の間」(値段発表後の意図的 pause)
  - 対象者の絞り込み (例: タブレット → 60歳以上シニアに明示)
- **Over-mimic**: **HIGH** — 長崎訛り high-pitch 易變 parody
- **Mitigation**: "anchor on structural craft (序破急 + 2秒間 + 対象絞込), not surface rhythm"
- **Cross-ref to zh-TW**: STRONG — 東森購物 / momo TV shopping direct parallel

### zh-TW Q1 center — 天下雜誌 CommonWealth

- **Era**: 1981 → present; 殷允芃 founding 總編輯
- **Primary sources**:
  - [cw.com.tw 封面故事 archive](https://www.cw.com.tw/)
  - 殷允芃《發現台灣》(1991, 天下雜誌, ISBN-anchored, NCL verifiable)
- **Voice signature**:
  - Macro-structural framing (「結構性」/「世代」/「重新定位」)
  - Data-anchored but humanities-inflected
  - Measured declarative; rarely interrogative / imperative
  - Long sentence rhythm, semi-colon/dash cadence
- **Over-mimic**: MEDIUM — "generic TW business-magazine voice" risk; anchor on 殷允芃-era specifically

### zh-TW Q1 center — 報導者 The Reporter (center register subset)

- **Era**: 2015 founded (何榮幸); non-profit, CC-licensed open access
- **Critical boundary**: 報導者 has TWO distinct registers — 調查報導 extreme (Q1-Q2 edge with emotional cost) vs 解釋性新聞 center. **Only the CENTER register fills Q1-center slot.**
- **Primary sources**:
  - [twreporter.org](https://www.twreporter.org/)
- **Voice signature**:
  - Explicitly sourced (「根據我們的訪談」/「經比對...資料」)
  - Structural framing w/ investigative specificity
  - 多方交叉驗證 disclosure
  - Restrained emotion — powerful by understatement
- **Over-mimic**: LOW-MEDIUM — distinctive from 天下 analytical

### EN Q2 extreme — Extinction Rebellion "Declaration of Rebellion"

- **Era**: 2018-10-31 launch (Parliament Square) → active
- **Creator**: Movement co-founders (Gail Bradbrook, Roger Hallam, Simon Bramwell); collective, not agency
- **Primary sources**:
  - [extinctionrebellion.uk/declaration/](https://extinctionrebellion.uk/declaration/)
  - *This Is Not A Drill: An Extinction Rebellion Handbook* (Penguin 2019, ISBN 978-0141991443)
- **Verbatim signature lines**:
  - "We hold the following to be true:"
  - "We refuse to bequeath a dying planet to future generations by failing to act now."
  - "We act in peace, with ferocious love of these lands in our hearts. We act on behalf of life."
  - "It is our duty to act."
- **Voice signature**:
  - Declarative scripture register (echoes US Declaration of Independence)
  - Aphoristic density (every sentence freestanding-quotable)
  - Civic-legalistic + lyrical ("bequeath", "wilful complicity", "ferocious love")
  - First-person-plural as moral collective
- **Over-mimic**: **HIGH** — Declaration-of-Independence pastiche is LLM default for "manifesto"
- **Mitigation**: "civic-declarative register — do NOT use for commercial product copy; this is civic-movement register"

### EN Q2 extreme — Nike "Dream Crazy/Crazier"

- **Era**: 2018 "Dream Crazy" (Kaepernick) → 2019 "Dream Crazier" (Serena Williams Oscars)
- **Agency**: Wieden+Kennedy Portland (CCOs Ponte + O'Rourke)
- **Primary sources**:
  - [wk.com/work/nike-dream-crazy/](https://www.wk.com/work/nike-dream-crazy/)
  - [wk.com/work/nike-dream-crazier/](https://www.wk.com/work/nike-dream-crazier/)
  - Ad Age "Best of 2019"; Emmy Outstanding Commercial 2019
- **Verbatim lines**:
  - "Believe in something. Even if it means sacrificing everything."
  - "Don't ask if your dreams are crazy. Ask if they're crazy enough."
  - "A woman running a marathon was crazy. A woman boxing was crazy. A woman dunking? Crazy."
- **Voice signature**:
  - Anaphora (repeated "If we…" / "A woman…") — pulpit rhetoric borrowed
  - Single-word escalation punctuation ("Crazy." "Nuts." "Delusional.")
  - Word-reclamation (crazy → virtue)
- **Over-mimic**: **HIGH** — "Believe in something. Even if it means sacrificing everything." is most-copied post-2018 brand structure
- **Intensity differentiation**: existing "Just Do It" = 3-word imperative (center); "Dream Crazy" = extended anaphoric sermon (extreme). Same brand, 2 steps more extreme. NOT redundant — but careful not to over-concentrate on Nike

### EN Q3 extreme — Steak-umm / Nathan Allebach era

- **Era**: 2017 account takeover → Dec 2021 Allebach step-down
- **Creator**: Nathan Allebach via Allebach Communications (Quakertown, PA)
- **Primary sources**:
  - [Fast Company Apr 2020 profile](https://www.fastcompany.com/90487288/how-steak-umm-yes-that-steak-umm-became-a-voice-of-reason-in-the-pandemic)

  > "why are so many young people flocking to brands on social media for love, guidance, and attention? I'll tell you why. they're isolated from real communities, working service jobs they hate while barely making ends meat… they go to memes. they go to obscure or absurdist humor. they go to frozen meat companies on twitter."
- **Voice signature**:
  - Lowercase-only + long-form confessional (formal punctuation abandoned)
  - Phenomenological-essay register smuggled into product-tweet
  - Self-aware 4th-wall ("frozen meat companies on twitter")
  - Millennial-existential (loneliness, epistemology, mental health routine)
- **Over-mimic**: MEDIUM — register hard to imitate without seeming pretentious (protective)
- **Intensity differentiation**: unique — NOT Oatly's activist-earnest / NOT Duolingo's chaotic-threatening / NOT Innocent's cheerful / NOT Waller-Bridge's literary-cinematic. Fills "peer-intimate that drops brand-mask mid-sentence"

## Open-flagged issues for consolidation

1. **全聯 re-classification**: zh-TW agent flagged 全聯 TV-era 2006-2014 is Q3-**center** not extreme (格言 aphorism register, not peer-intimate). Earlier brand-era research did not differentiate. Consolidation must fix.

2. **Kurashicom Q3/Q4 boundary**: 北欧、暮らしの道具店 has two layers — warmth/narrative essays (Q3) vs product-how-to (Q4). Anchor definition must explicitly draw boundary; failing to do so will cause voice leakage.

3. **高田明 vs 高田旭人 era boundary**: ジャパネットたかた anchor ONLY covers founder era (1990-2015). Son succession era is different register.

4. **Reuters / Bloomberg Japan localization caveat**: these are international wires with JP localization, not pure JP-native anchors. If strict JP-native Q1-extreme needed, fall back to 日経社説 (but carries residual warmth).

5. **Duolingo 2021-2022 window discipline**: post-2023 viral-cliché era should NOT be anchor; use formative window only.

6. **XR Declaration register-scope**: "civic-declarative" register — explicit do-NOT-use-for-commercial warning required.

7. **Nike stacking risk**: existing "Just Do It" center + new "Dream Crazy" extreme = two Nike anchors. Valid (different rhetorical machinery) but consolidation should ensure not over-concentrated.

8. **白書 caveat**: JP government register flagged HIGH over-mimic; do NOT include as active anchor, only reference.
