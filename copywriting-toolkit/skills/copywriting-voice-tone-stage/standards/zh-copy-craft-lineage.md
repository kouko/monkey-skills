# ZH Copy Craft Lineage — 許舜英 / 李欣頻 / 葉明桂

Tier 3 standard: fully self-contained. Deepens the voice master
references introduced in
`copywriting-voice-quadrant-stage/standards/voice-quadrant-positioning.md`
§Q2 ZH anchors. Where the positioning canon provides 2-3 line voice
summaries per brand, this standard provides full voice analysis:
representative corpus, stylistic grammar patterns, generational
context, and — critically — the specific LLM reproduction gap for each
master.

Three masters, one generation cohort: 許舜英 (意識形態廣告, active
1988–2008), 李欣頻 (誠品文案 1990s–2000s), 葉明桂 (奧美 1984–, 左岸
咖啡館 1990s–). All three define Taiwan's Q2 literary-reflective pole
of Chinese short-copy voice, which remains the dominant reference when
ZH brands reach for "文青" / "意識形態" / "品味 retail" registers.

## Critical Attribution Corrections

- **Drift #Z1: 「我不在咖啡館，就在去咖啡館的路上」is NOT original
  to 葉明桂 / 奧美.** The phrase is a Chinese adaptation of Austrian
  poet Peter Altenberg's (1859–1919) Viennese saying
  "Wenn der Altenberg nicht im Kaffeehaus ist, ist er am Weg dorthin"
  (「如果 Altenberg 不在咖啡館，就在前往咖啡館的路上」). Originally
  said **about** Altenberg by his contemporaries (not by him), later
  recirculated as self-description. 台灣奧美 sinicized and adopted
  it for 左岸咖啡館 — a borrowed line, not original composition. The
  voice-positioning canon already flags this ("borrowed from Peter
  Altenberg, sinicized"); repeating here to prevent drift into
  claiming 葉明桂 coined it.

- **Drift #Z2: 「拋開書本到街上去」series (誠品敦南) is 李欣頻, but
  the book title it inverts — 《書を捨てよ、町へ出よう》(1967) — is
  寺山修司's.** Some secondary sources omit the 寺山修司 attribution
  and present the copy as pure 李欣頻 invention. 李欣頻 herself has
  documented the 寺山修司 inspiration in interviews; do not flatten
  the lineage.

- **Drift #Z3: 意識形態廣告 (Icey) founding year is 1987, not 1988.**
  許舜英 and 鄭松茂 co-founded the agency in 1987. The 中興百貨
  campaign run began 1988 and peaked through 1999. 許舜英 departed
  意識形態 in 2008 to join 奧美時尚 (Ogilvy Fashion & Lifestyle).
  中興百貨 itself closed its last store in 2008. The 1988–1999
  decade is the ideology-era canonical window — post-2000 中興百貨
  copy is not canonical 許舜英 voice.

- **Drift #Z4: Widely-circulated online "中興百貨 文案" lists mix
  許舜英 works with later imitations and uncredited copy.** The
  confirmed canonical set is anchored in 《許舜英購物日記》+
  意識形態 award-year archives (時報廣告金像獎, 4A 自由創意獎,
  亞太廣告獎). Lines not traceable to these sources should be
  treated as [disputed] pending primary-source confirmation.

- **Drift #Z5: 「多喝水 MORE WATER」(金車) is NOT 吳念真's work.**
  It is 奧美台灣 in-house copy, frequently misattributed to 吳念真
  online due to his 1990s TW advertising prominence. 吳念真's
  canonical TW advertising voice anchors are **保力達B 口白**
  (李奧貝納, 1990s–ongoing, 台語 register) + **全國電子「足感心」**
  (2000s). Do not list 多喝水 among 吳念真 works.

- **Drift #Z6: 孫大偉's agency affiliation is 台灣奧美 (initially)
  then 偉太廣告 (1991+ independent founder).** Some secondary sources
  incorrectly cite him as 智威湯遜 (J. Walter Thompson). 孫大偉 worked
  at 奧美 1980s–1990s before founding 偉太; JWT attribution is an
  error to reject at source-check.

- **Drift #Z7: 長榮航空〈I SEE YOU〉(2013) 旁白 = 金城武 本人獨白,
  NOT 吳念真.** The ad stars Takeshi Kaneshiro and the monologue
  「我心中，尚未崩壞的地方」is delivered by Kaneshiro himself.
  吳念真 did not participate in this campaign's VO. When citing
  吳念真's iconic 旁白 work, stick to 保力達B / 全國電子 / other
  confirmed attributions.

- **Drift #Z8: 全聯經濟美學 (2015–) creative lead = 龔大中
  (台灣奧美 ECD at campaign start, 2020 promoted to CCO).** Some
  online sources incorrectly cite 林敬凱 as creative lead, OR
  mistakenly credit 邱彥翔 as copywriter. **邱彥翔 is the actor
  ("全聯先生")** hired 2006 for the pre-經濟美學 TV-era campaigns;
  he is not a copywriter and did not author the 2015+ 經濟美學
  aphoristic copy (「長得漂亮是本錢，把錢花得漂亮是本事。」etc.).
  Primary-source anchor: 數位時代 2015 龔大中 專訪 + 經理人月刊
  2020 全聯 × 奧美 對談 + 第38屆時報廣告金像獎 全聯經濟美學 2015
  得獎紀錄.

## Generational Context

All three masters emerged from **1980s–1990s 台灣解嚴後新都會消費主義
覺醒**. This era's key features:

- 1987 解嚴 → 1990s 文化開放 + 消費升級 + 女性經濟獨立
- 意識形態廣告公司 (1987–2008) as the cultural focal point for "有
  態度的消費" — advertising as cultural criticism, not product
  description
- 誠品書店 1989 敦南店 → 1995 遷址 → 2000s 全島連鎖, anchoring a
  "書店 = 生活提案" brand category that did not exist before
- 台灣奧美 under 莊淑芬 / 葉明桂 leadership institutionalizing
  strategic-planning-first advertising ("不是賣產品，是經營品牌")

The generational context matters because these voices emerged from a
media environment where **a full-page 報紙廣告 or one-minute TVC had
to carry an entire cultural thesis**. There was no landing page, no
retargeting, no A/B test. The reader was hailed as a **literate
cultural peer**, not a conversion target. This constraint produced
copy that privileged aphoristic density, cross-domain vocabulary
transplant, and cultural-reference-dense hooks over specificity and
call-to-action clarity.

Applying these voices uncritically to 2020s performance-marketing
contexts will produce beautiful copy that does not convert. Use them
as voice reference, not as targeting strategy.

## 許舜英 (意識形態廣告, active 1988–2008)

### Career arc

共同創辦人 + ECD, 意識形態廣告公司 (1987-2008). 2008 加入奧美
(Ogilvy Fashion & Lifestyle). 中興百貨 campaign run 1988-1999 pushed
the store's revenue ~28× over the decade (Brain magazine documented
multiple TCC / 時報廣告金像獎 wins). Also anchored 司迪麥口香糖, 中國
時報 campaigns during same era. Published author: 《許舜英購物日記》
(漫遊者文化, 2011), 《我不是一本型錄》文集 + 《古著文本》.

### Representative works (10+ confirmed, 中興百貨 1988–1999)

- 「服裝就是一種高明的政治，政治就是一種高明的服裝。」— 中興百貨
  貴族與侍女篇 / 貴族與侍者篇
- 「到服裝店培養氣質，到書店展示服裝。但不論如何你都該想想，有了
  胸部之後，你還需要什麼？腦袋。」— 中興百貨 1998 春裝上市 書店篇
  (attested in 數英 archive; widely reprinted)
- 「禁慾是虛妄的說法，只要是黑色，你就無法抵抗。」— 中興百貨
  意識形態篇
- 「衣服是這個時代最後的美好環境。」— 中興百貨 森林篇 / 櫻花篇
- 「流行是安全的，風格是危險的。」— 中興百貨 危險篇
- 「沒有服裝就沒有性。」— 中興百貨 梅蘭芳篇
- 「認真地使用台北，將讓你重新理解台北。」— 中興百貨 絕對在地篇
- 「女性主義就是失敗在愛情和衣服這兩件事情上。」— 中興百貨 (cited
  in voice-quadrant-positioning canonical corpus)
- 「不論年代，戀愛是一回事，結婚是另一回事。」— 中興百貨 結婚進行
  曲篇
- 「三日不購物便覺靈魂可憎。」— 中興百貨 三日不購物便覺靈魂可憎篇
- 「只要懂得買，連港式素蠔油也會分泌亞洲美學精神。」— 中興百貨
  (same campaign)

### Voice signature

| Dimension | Characteristic | Example |
|-----------|---------------|---------|
| Definitional inversion | 「X 是一種 Y」aphoristic equation | 「服裝就是一種高明的政治」— equates fashion with statecraft, then flips it |
| Cross-domain vocabulary transplant | Imports 政治 / 哲學 / 姿態 / 語法 / 命題 / 辯證 vocabulary into consumer copy | 「女性主義」appearing in a department-store ad |
| Noun-phrase density | Ends on noun / abstract concept, not verb / action | 「腦袋。」— closes with a noun, not a buy-now |
| Paradox / negation hook | 「不是 X, 是 Y」or the negation comes first | 「禁慾是虛妄的說法」— opens by rejecting the frame |
| Cultural-reference density | Assumes reader recognizes 梅蘭芳 / 侍女 / 祖母衣櫃 / 港式素蠔油 as signifiers | Copy reads as 散文, not as pitch |

### Stylistic grammar patterns

- Full-length declarative sentences (rarely fragments — the opposite
  of 糸井's style)
- Sentence-final 。 carrying philosophical weight, never exclamatory
- Enumerated second-person address ("你還需要什麼？") used as
  rhetorical hook, not as actual question
- Colon / comma chains that build essayistic rhythm ("X，Y。Z，Y。")
- Zero diminutives, zero emoji-adjacent register — the voice refuses
  cuteness as a posture

### LLM reproduction gap

LLM output attempting 許舜英 voice typically **produces hollow
definitional inversions** — "X 是一種 Y" frames that sound aphoristic
but carry no cultural critique payload. The surface grammar is easy
to copy; the cultural tension underneath is not.

Specific failure modes:
- Generating 「X 就是一種 Y」inversions where X and Y are both
  bland consumer categories (e.g. "咖啡就是一種生活"), missing the
  original's politics / power / gender friction
- Over-use of 「你需要什麼？XX。」self-answered questions (one per
  piece in 許舜英 canon, LLMs produce three)
- Flattening cultural references to generic "literary" gestures —
  e.g. referring to "書" instead of naming 寺山修司 / 阿莫多瓦
- Producing the declaration without the paradox — 許舜英's signature
  is assertion followed by inversion, not assertion alone

**Mitigation**: before drafting, require the writer to name
(a) a specific cultural tension the target reader actually feels
and (b) a specific second concept to transplant (from politics /
philosophy / arts). If the inversion X/Y collapses into synonyms,
reject the draft. Canonical 許舜英 sentences all contain a
**power-disparity word** (政治 / 統治 / 殖民 / 失敗 / 禁慾 /
危險) — if the draft is missing it, voice is off.

## 李欣頻 (誠品書店, 1990s–2000s)

### Career arc

1990s 敦南誠品應徵案 (二十多歲) 開啟十餘年誠品文案生涯. 《誠品副
作用》(新新聞文化, 1998) 成為第一本著作 — 六個月內躍升文學類 #11
暢銷. 後續出版 《廣告副作用．藝文篇》、《繼續字戀》、《李欣頻的
廣告四庫全書》(時報文化, 2016) 收錄 1991–2016 文案作品. 2000s–2020s
轉向寫作教學 / 創意課程 (《十四堂人生創意課》系列 + 《李欣頻的寫作
之道》2019).

### Representative works (10+ confirmed, 誠品書店 1990s–2000s)

- 「所有創建一座書店的慾望，都即將在這裡發生。」— 誠品敦南店
  看不見的書店 / 搬遷前預告篇 (~1995)
- 「送舊迎新．移館別戀」— 誠品敦南店 搬遷活動 (~1995)
- 「九九九九種繁衍生活的創意方式」— 誠品敦南店 新館開幕 (~1995)
- 「以慾望別，而非物品別分類」— 誠品 品牌定位主張 (1989-1995 期間
  反覆出現)
- 「拋開阿莫多瓦的高跟鞋到街上去。拋開村上春樹的彈珠遊戲到街上
  去。拋開徐四金的香水到街上去。拋開彼得梅爾的普羅旺斯到街上去。」
  — 誠品敦南店「拋開書本到街上去」系列, 致敬 寺山修司
  《書を捨てよ、町へ出よう》(1967)
- 「在書與非書之間，我們歡迎各種可能的閱讀者。」— 誠品《閱讀者
  的群像》篇
- 「我在閱讀中，遇見另一個自己。」— 誠品書店 canonical
  (attested in voice-quadrant-positioning)

### Voice signature

| Dimension | Characteristic | Example |
|-----------|---------------|---------|
| 排比枚舉 (parallel enumeration) | Lists of four / five / seven variants building rhythm | 「拋開阿莫多瓦... 拋開村上春樹... 拋開徐四金... 拋開彼得梅爾...」 |
| 具名引用 (named reference) | Specific authors / artists / books as signifiers | 寺山修司 / 阿莫多瓦 / 徐四金 / 彼得梅爾 / 村上春樹 |
| 抽象動作詞提列 (abstract-verb foregrounding) | Verbs like 繁衍 / 別戀 / 拋開 carry emotional weight usually reserved for nouns | 「九九九九種繁衍生活」— 繁衍 is biological, applied to lifestyle |
| 量詞遊戲 (classifier play) | Unusual classifiers / numbers ("九九九九種") used as poetic device | Not "many ways", specifically "九九九九" |
| 讀者地位反轉 (reader-as-cultural-peer) | Treats reader as already literate, never as someone to be educated | No "let us tell you about..." framing |

### Stylistic grammar patterns

- Opening with 所有 / 每一 / 在 prepositional frames that establish
  universe before content
- Comma cadence heavier than 許舜英 — breathing rhythm over
  aphoristic density
- Noun-phrase endings common (「...的可能」「...的方式」) — event-
  space nouns rather than closed statements
- Verb choice prefers 繁衍 / 遇見 / 歡迎 / 展開 / 別戀 over 買 /
  選 / 試 / 享

### LLM reproduction gap

LLM output attempting 李欣頻 voice typically **produces listy parallel
structures without the named-reference payload** — the enumeration
works as rhythm but the items are generic ("小說... 詩... 散文..."
instead of "阿莫多瓦... 村上春樹... 徐四金...").

Specific failure modes:
- Generic enumeration items (文學 / 藝術 / 音樂) where 李欣頻 would
  name specific authors
- Missing the antecedent — 李欣頻's copy always sits in dialogue with
  an existing cultural work (寺山修司, Peter Altenberg's unwritten
  counterpart). LLM output invents lists without an anchor work.
- Treating 「繁衍」「別戀」as decorative adjectives instead of load-
  bearing verbs
- Resolving ambiguity — 李欣頻's 慾望別分類 leaves "慾望" open; LLMs
  want to define it

**Mitigation**: before drafting, require the writer to name
(a) one antecedent work or figure the copy is in dialogue with and
(b) four specific named signifiers the target reader would recognize.
If the draft reads fine when those four names are replaced by generic
words, the voice has collapsed. Canonical 李欣頻 enumeration always
passes the "remove one name, does the rhythm break" test — the
specificity is load-bearing.

## 葉明桂 (奧美廣告, 1984–; 左岸咖啡館 1998–)

### Career arc

1984 加入國泰建業廣告 (奧美前身), 從業務企劃起步. 現任奧美副董事長
兼奧美集團策略長. Fairfield University (Connecticut) 傳播碩士.
左岸咖啡館 (統一企業委託) 為 1990s 代表策略作品, 品牌延續超過 20
年 (至 2020s). 文案撰寫由台灣奧美團隊執行, 策略方向由葉明桂定調.
著作：《品牌的技術和藝術：向廣告鬼才葉明桂學洞察力與故事力》
(天下文化, 2017; ISBN 9789864792375) — 此書為奧美策略派 Q1 方法的
canonical reference. 橫跨 50+ 品牌 (含左岸咖啡館 / 統一 / 台灣高
鐵 / 全聯 / 麥斯威爾等).

### Voice signature

| Dimension | Characteristic | Example |
|-----------|---------------|---------|
| 場景敘事 (scene-over-slogan) | Copy paints a scene, the product appears inside the scene — not the other way around | 「我在這裡，找到一個角落，一個上午，一杯 Cafe au lait」— scene first, product (Cafe au lait) embedded |
| 異國氛圍借景 (foreign-atmosphere licensing) | Imports Parisian / Viennese / literary-European aesthetic as brand universe | 蕭邦 / 西蒙波娃 / 達文西 / 雨果 as recurring signifiers |
| 常客文化 (regular-customer intimacy) | Reader positioned as returning habitué, not new visitor | 「明天見」篇 — French 「À demain」as habit signal |
| 時間緩拍 (slow-tempo temporal frame) | 一個上午 / 一輩子 / 春天的最後一天 — elongated time expressions | 「花一輩子的時間，趕時間。」— paradoxical time-frame |
| 策略先行，文案後發 (strategy-first, copy-second) | Voice emerges from product-category-reframe decision, not from clever wording | 左岸 brief: 「不是賣咖啡，是經營咖啡館」— strategy drives copy |

### Stylistic grammar patterns

- First-person 我 POV common (unlike 許舜英 / 李欣頻 who prefer
  impersonal register)
- Short sentence + short sentence + long closing sentence rhythm
- Concrete sensory anchors (杯 / 角落 / 上午 / 雨 / 燈 / 音樂) in
  every piece
- Low reliance on aphorism — the signature is atmospheric, not
  epigrammatic
- Place names / object names in dense specific detail (Cafe au lait
  not 咖啡, 聖拉查爾 not 火車站)

### LLM reproduction gap

LLM output attempting 葉明桂 voice (or more precisely, the 台灣奧美
左岸 style) typically **produces generic café-atmosphere copy without
the strategic category-reframe underneath**. The surface is
atmospheric prose; the underlying "not selling coffee, running a
café" thesis is missing — so the atmosphere does not build a brand,
it just decorates a product.

Specific failure modes:
- Writing a café scene without specifying the café's cultural universe
  (Parisian vs. Viennese vs. Brooklyn matters; LLMs default to
  generic "cozy café")
- Missing the first-person habitué voice — LLMs default to second-
  person address ("when you walk into our café...")
- Losing the slow tempo — LLMs accelerate toward conversion ("try
  our new blend"), 葉明桂 / 左岸 holds the frame open
- Producing atmosphere without strategy — the copy is pretty but the
  brand is not built, because the category-reframe question ("what
  are we actually selling?") was skipped

**Mitigation**: before drafting, require the writer to answer two
strategic questions (per 葉明桂's method in 《品牌的技術和藝術》):
(1) What is this brand actually selling that the competitor is not?
(left bank answer: not coffee, but access to a Parisian literary
state of being) (2) What does a returning customer's relationship to
this brand look like — daily ritual, weekly visit, life milestone?
Only after these are answered can the scene copy be written. Without
them, atmospheric copy becomes interchangeable between brands.

## LLM Reproduction Gaps — Cross-master Summary

| Master | Hardest dimension to reproduce | Canonical failure mode | Single best mitigation gate |
|--------|-------------------------------|------------------------|-----------------------------|
| 許舜英 | 文化批判 payload underneath definitional inversion | Hollow 「X 是一種 Y」without power-disparity word | Require a power-disparity word (政治/殖民/失敗/禁慾/危險) in the sentence |
| 李欣頻 | Named-reference specificity in enumeration | Generic-list rhythm without load-bearing names | Require 4 specific named cultural figures; confirm removing one breaks the rhythm |
| 葉明桂 / 左岸 | Strategic category-reframe underneath atmospheric prose | Pretty scene without brand thesis | Require answering "what are we actually selling that competitor is not?" before drafting scene |

All three masters share a common LLM-failure pattern: **surface
grammar is easy to copy; the cultural / strategic / critical payload
underneath is not**. ZH Q2 literary voice is defined by payload +
grammar in tension; reproducing grammar alone produces what Chinese
copywriting critics call 「文青腔」— a voice that sounds like the
canon but means nothing.

## Voice selection guidance

When `copywriting-voice-quadrant-stage/standards/voice-quadrant-positioning.md`
references a Q2 ZH voice, or when `copywriting-voice-tone-stage`
calibrates ZH tone, use this standard to pick the right master:

| Voice reference | Best fit | Avoid when |
|-----------------|----------|------------|
| 許舜英 | Fashion / luxury retail / cultural-criticism-tolerant brands; audiences comfortable with provocation | Mass-market FMCG; youth-targeted short-form; any context where power-disparity vocabulary reads as alienating |
| 李欣頻 | Bookstores / lifestyle retail / cultural institutions; audiences who read named references | Utility products; price-sensitive campaigns; audiences without literary-reference capital |
| 葉明桂 / 左岸 | Long-arc brand-building for premium consumer goods with strategic category-reframe potential | Performance-marketing contexts requiring measurable CTR; short-term promotional windows |

For copy audit (`copy-audit.md` in `copywriting-audit-stage`): when
diagnosing voice inconsistency, check against these signatures. A
piece claiming 許舜英 voice but missing power-disparity vocabulary
is misaligned. A piece claiming 李欣頻 voice but enumerating generic
categories instead of named references is collapsed. A piece
claiming 左岸 voice but with second-person CTA inside the scene is
voice-contaminated.

## Anti-Patterns

- **Attributing 「我不在咖啡館，就在去咖啡館的路上」to 葉明桂 as
  original composition**. It is a sinicized adaptation of Peter
  Altenberg's Viennese saying. The voice-quadrant-positioning canon
  flags this; do not drift.
- **Attributing unsourced "中興百貨 文案" to 許舜英**. Widely-
  shared lists online mix her canonical work with later imitations.
  Stick to sources traceable to 《許舜英購物日記》 + 意識形態
  award-year archives.
- **Treating 李欣頻's enumeration style as interchangeable with
  generic listing**. Her lists contain **load-bearing named
  signifiers** — the specificity is the voice. Remove the names and
  the voice is gone.
- **Mixing two ZH masters' voices in one piece**. Their registers
  conflict: 許舜英's aphoristic density overwhelms 李欣頻's breathing
  rhythm; 葉明桂 / 左岸's first-person intimacy collapses 許舜英's
  impersonal critique stance. One voice per piece.
- **Using 許舜英's definitional inversion for products without
  cultural-critique capacity**. A 民生用品 ad written as "X 就是
  一種 Y" reads as pretentious, not insightful. The inversion
  requires a cultural tension to inversely mirror — if the product
  category has no such tension, the form does not apply.
- **Applying 李欣頻's named-reference enumeration to audiences
  without literary-reference capital**. The voice assumes the
  reader recognizes 寺山修司 / 阿莫多瓦 / 徐四金. For audiences
  outside that reference set, the copy reads as cultural gatekeeping
  rather than invitation.
- **Reproducing 左岸 atmospheric prose without the strategic
  category-reframe underneath**. Atmosphere without brand thesis
  produces interchangeable café copy. 葉明桂's method in
  《品牌的技術和藝術》is explicit: strategy first, copy second.
- **Ignoring the generational context**. These voices emerged from
  1990s–2000s pre-digital media — full-page 報紙廣告, one-minute
  TVCs, no retargeting, no A/B. Applying them to 2020s performance-
  marketing contexts produces beautiful copy that does not convert.
  Use them as voice reference, not as targeting strategy.
- **Claiming LLM can "寫出 許舜英 / 李欣頻 / 左岸 style"**. LLM
  reproduces grammar but consistently fails on payload (cultural
  critique / named-reference specificity / strategic category-
  reframe). Use the mitigation gates per master, not the claim.
