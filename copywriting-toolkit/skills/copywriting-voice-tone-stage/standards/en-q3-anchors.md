## Overview

Q3 = Affinity × Emotion. EN canonical: MailChimp help center (one-voice-multiple-tones origin), Innocent Drinks (wackaging pioneer, UK DTC Q3 template), Oatly (activist edge), Liquid Death (punk edge), Waller-Bridge Fleabag (literary-cinematic confessional), Steak-umm Allebach-era (unique phenomenological-peer voice), Duolingo Parvez 2021-22 formative (chaotic-mascot-threat).
