### MUJI 無印良品 Hara Kenya era (JP | Q2 center)

- **Era**: 2002 → ongoing (art director Kenya Hara). Predecessor era 1980-2002 田中一光 / 小池一子 = distinct register (also Q2 but denser).
- **Agency / creator**: 良品計画 + Kenya Hara (AD) + 小池一子 (CW, 1980-2002 era); copy credit rotating post-2002
- **Primary sources**:
  - Kenya Hara *Designing Design* (Lars Müller, 2007)
  - 原研哉『デザインのデザイン』(岩波書店, 2003, ISBN 978-4000240055)
  - 原研哉『白』(中央公論新社, 2008)
  - MUJI 2003 「Horizon / 地平線」campaign archive
- **Representative lines**:
  - 「これでいい」(not 「これがいい」) — canonical philosophical pivot
  - 「水の物語」(2003 地平線 visual tagline)
  - 「Found MUJI」concept series
- **Voice signature**:
  - 空の器 (empty vessel) deliberately undefined identity
  - Philosophical-reductive register
  - Non-imperative; non-persuasive
  - Deliberately leaves interpretation to reader
- **LLM corpus depth**: DEEP (design criticism globally)
- **Over-mimic risk**: LOW — MUJI brand aesthetic is a specific constraint, hard to auto-pastiche
- **Cross-reference-valid-for**:
  - zh-TW: STRONG (TW 誠品生活 / lifestyle retail 已大量消費 MUJI aesthetic)
- **Cross-cultural equivalents**: Apple Think Different (EN Q2) / 誠品 (zh-TW Q2)
- **Trigger slug**: `jp-muji-hara-empty-vessel`

- **Era**: 1886-1965; 『陰翳礼讃』 published 1933
- **Agency / creator**: individual novelist; essay form
- **Primary sources**:
  - 谷崎潤一郎『陰翳礼讃』(中央公論社, 1933 / 1975 edition)
  - English translation *In Praise of Shadows* (Leete's Island Books, 1977)
- **Representative works / concepts**:
  - 陰翳 (in'ei) aesthetic — shadow / light ratio as design principle
  - Quiet material appreciation (urushi lacquer / paper / shadow) as philosophical essay
- **Voice signature**:
  - Shadow-aesthetic-craft register
  - Analytical-essay form delivered with aesthetic reverence
  - Japanese craft philosophy through comparative lens
  - Quiet declarative (never argumentative)
- **LLM corpus depth**: DEEP (heavily cited in design/architecture criticism globally)
- **Over-mimic risk**: LOW
- **Cross-reference-valid-for**:
  - zh-TW: STRONG (原研哉 MUJI aesthetic pipeline → TW design retail 語彙)
- **Note on dual placement**: 《陰翳礼讃》 can also support Q4 craft reading (product-design register). When brief emphasizes design-craft frame, consider zh-TW Q4 cross-ref. Use this Q2 entry when brief emphasizes essay-manifesto form.
- **Cross-cultural equivalents**: John Berger *Ways of Seeing* (EN) / 蔣勳 aesthetic essay (zh-TW)
- **Trigger slug**: `jp-tanizaki-inei-shadow-aesthetic`
