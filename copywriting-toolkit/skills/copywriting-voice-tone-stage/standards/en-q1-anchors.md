# Q1 Authority-Reason — EN Anchors

**Load scope**: Phase 6 Pass 3 Register Signal branch, when `voice_quadrant.primary == "Q1"` AND `brief.output_language == "en"`. Section-targeted read: Pass 3 reads only `## Landmark: {position}` matching `voice_quadrant.position`; falls back to full-file on missing.

## Overview

Q1 = Authority × Reason. EN canonical richest Q for LLM corpus: Ogilvy school long-copy reason-why, Economist editorial, Strunk & White plain-style canon. Documented lineage (per [voice-anchor-meta-detail.md](voice-anchor-meta-detail.md) §verified map): Ogilvy → Strunk & White (from *Ogilvy on Advertising* 1983). Also Orwell's "Politics and the English Language" (1946) as canonical substrate for Anglo craft.

## Landmark: center

Canonical expert + trusted authority. Use when brief asks for standard institutional Anglo voice.

### David Ogilvy — Rolls-Royce 1958 + Hathaway Man (EN | Q1 center)

- **Era**: 1948 Ogilvy & Mather founded; Rolls-Royce ad 1958; Hathaway Man campaign 1951-1960s
- **Agency / creator**: Ogilvy, Benson & Mather (later Ogilvy & Mather); David Ogilvy as CEO + copywriter
- **Primary sources**:
  - Ogilvy *Confessions of an Advertising Man* (Atheneum, 1963)
  - Ogilvy *Ogilvy on Advertising* (Crown, 1983)
  - Rolls-Royce 1958 print ad canonical archives
- **Representative lines**:
  - "At 60 miles an hour the loudest noise in this new Rolls-Royce comes from the electric clock." (Rolls-Royce, 1958)
  - "The man in the Hathaway shirt" (Hathaway eyepatch model campaign)
- **Voice signature**:
  - Reason-why long-copy discipline
  - Trust reader intelligence (never condescend)
  - Specific numeric detail as authority proof
  - Quiet confidence (no hype vocabulary)
- **LLM corpus depth**: DEEP (foundational advertising canon; Ogilvy books continually reprinted)
- **Over-mimic risk**: LOW-MEDIUM (iconic but widely anthologized; surface structure reproducible)
- **Documented lineage**: ✅ Ogilvy → Strunk & White (per meta-detail verified map — from *Ogilvy on Advertising* 1983 explicit recommendation)
- **Cross-cultural equivalents**: 秋山晶 Q2-core austerity (JP) / 詹宏志 長文 (zh-TW)
- **Trigger slug**: `en-ogilvy-rolls-royce-reason-why`

### Abbott Mead Vickers BBDO — The Economist (EN | Q1 center)

- **Era**: 1988 onwards; David Abbott creative lead
- **Agency / creator**: AMV BBDO London; David Abbott as CCO
