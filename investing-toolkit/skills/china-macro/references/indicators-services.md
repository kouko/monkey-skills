# Services / 服务业

---

## services-production-yoy: 服务业生产指数 当月同比

- **Series code**: NBS cid `2eb7c0ebd79a4225887d5ea68c7aed4c` / indicator `3fc5439f03ec46a5a76e8032036e8c17`
- **Source**: NBS direct (月度→服务业生产指数)
- **Unit**: Percent (%)
- **Frequency**: Monthly
- **Publication lag**: ~15 days after month-end (published with 三大数据)
- **History**: From 2017 when NBS introduced the composite measure

**What it measures**: Year-over-year percentage change in the Services
Production Index — an output gauge for the services sector analogous
to Industrial Value Added for manufacturing. Covers transportation,
warehousing, postal, information services, financial services, real
estate services, leasing, business services, scientific research,
technical services, and more.

**How to interpret**:
- Rising → service sector expanding; supports CSI 300 services-heavy
  names (insurers, brokers, tech platforms).
- Falling → services drag on GDP; typical in 2020 COVID shock, 2022
  Shanghai lockdown.
- Compare with **non-manufacturing PMI** (`pmi-non-manufacturing`):
  PMI leads by 1-2 months but is survey-based; this is quantitative
  output.
- Compare with **retail-yoy**: retail measures goods only (mostly);
  this measures services separately. The goods-vs-services split has
  been a consistent 2024-2026 macro theme.

**Market significance**: ⭐⭐
Second-tier indicator but valuable because the services sector is now
>55% of GDP and can diverge meaningfully from industrial output. Used
by macro analysts to decompose GDP "beats vs misses" into
goods-production vs services.

**When to use**: GDP decomposition, services-recovery narrative, pair
trading (services-heavy vs industrials-heavy equities).

**China-specific context**:
- Created in 2017 as NBS recognised that 三驾马车 framework undersampled
  services. Parallels the Japanese 第3次産業活動指数 (Tertiary Industry
  Activity Index).
- Post-COVID recovery narrative in 2023-2024 heavily referenced this
  series — services rebound outpaced goods retail.
- Jan-Feb combined release convention applies here too (NBS doesn't
  publish 当月 YoY for Jan or Feb individually; only 累计 Jan-Feb).

**Common pitfalls**:
- Services here includes REAL ESTATE services (brokerage, property
  management) — a sector under severe pressure 2022+. Drag from real
  estate services can hide otherwise-healthy services growth.
- The series is NOT directly comparable to US ISM Services PMI or
  China Caixin Services PMI — those are survey diffusion indices
  (50 = no change), this is output quantity YoY.
