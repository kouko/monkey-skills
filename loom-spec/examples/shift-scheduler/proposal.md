**Seed:** "Let a manager auto-generate a weekly staff schedule respecting
availability, skills, and labor rules."

Generated by loom-spec GENERATE (spec-expansion 3 phases) +

**Employee**

Protected-leave (FMLA/parental/jury/medical) excludes from assignment WITHOUT
counting as a fair-distribution penalty.

## Path Г— edge matrix

Phase в‘ў artifact. The cartesian grid `backbone Г— object Г— CTA Г— state`
over-generates (10 objects Г— ~6 CTAs Г— ~6 states Г— 10 steps в‰€ thousands of
cells, most illegal). Below is the grid **after** pruning through the 7 lenses:
illegal cells dropped, surviving legal paths kept, and aspects the grid cannot
see surfaced by the system/NFR/policy lenses.

### Lens 1 вЂ” state-transition legality (paths kept / edges flagged)
- **Kept (legal happy paths):** Schedule Draftв†’Generatingв†’Feasibleв†’UnderReviewв†’Publishedв†’Lockedв†’Archived; Shift Openв†’Assignedв†’Confirmedв†’InProgressв†’Completed; Assignment Proposedв†’Confirmedв†’Completed; SwapRequest Requestedв†’вЂ¦в†’Approvedв†’Applied.
- **Flagged as edge cases (legal but off-happy-path):** Generated:Infeasible (with explanation), Generated:Partial (gaps), Shift NoShow:Uncovered, Assignment Modified-revalidate-FAIL, SwapRequest Approved-validate-FAIL, Schedule un-publish/retraction, emergency unlock.
- **Dropped (impossible cells):** assign to a Terminated employee; Completed before clock-in; publish a Generating schedule; swap on a Locked/past shift; apply a SwapRequest that skips validate.

### Lens 2 вЂ” BVA (data edges)
zero employees В· zero shifts В· demand > total capacity (mathematically
infeasible) В· employee at exactly `max_hours` (off-by-one `<` vs `<=`) В·
fractional hours toward a cap В· negative/zero `required_headcount` В· shift
crossing midnight / the week boundary (hours-attribution rule) В· min-rest
across the week seam В· cert expiring mid-week / mid-shift В· availability for a
non-existent future week В· 14-day-notice at 13d 23h (boundary of the notice
window).

### Lens 3 вЂ” CRUD completeness (per object)
- **Create:** Shift from template/forecast; Assignment by engine/manager; SwapRequest by employee; Availability by employee.
- **Read:** scoped reads (manager sees own location only; employee sees published only; cost/DOB hidden); Auditor/Regulator export.
- **Update:** override Assignment (must revalidate); reopen/un-publish Schedule; supersede Availability/LaborRule.
- **Delete:** cancel Assignment (may understaff Shift); discard Schedule; retention-purge/anonymize PII vs statutory min-keep.

### Lens 4 вЂ” permissions (who may perform each CTA)
RBAC matrix: only Manager/role-X may assign, override, approve, publish, lock;
Employee may set own availability, request swap, confirm, decline; engine may
propose/validate; Auditor/Regulator read-only export. Unauthorized-actor paths
(employee edits another's availability; shift-lead publishes; manager overrides
a *statutory* minimum) are rejected or require dual-control.

### Lens 5 вЂ” empty / error / loading states
empty: no availability submitted, no shifts, no eligible staff. error:
GenerationFailed (solver crash/timeout), infeasible-with-explanation,
revalidate-FAIL, notification-delivery-Failed. loading: Generating
(long-running solve), partial-write-in-progress (must be atomic / not surfaced
as a valid partial result).

### Lens 6 вЂ” NFR (system-layer aspects OOUX cannot reach)
performance/scale (solve latency for N employees Г— M shifts; documented
ceiling) В· solver determinism (fixed seed в†’ diffable re-run) В· solver
time-budget/timeout (best-so-far + unproven-optimal flag) В· concurrency
(optimistic-lock on concurrent manager edits; engine-while-edit;
concurrent-swap double-booking) В· idempotent generate (dedupe double-trigger) В·
partial-write atomicity (all-or-nothing) В· DST / multi-timezone (compute
rest/notice in UTC; normalize cross-TZ swaps) В· privacy (cost, DOB are
sensitive) В· audit log (append-only, who/what/when, predictability-pay
evidence) В· observability (solve-time, infeasibility rate) В· i18n (week-start
Sun vs Mon, locale, currency) В· a11y (status not by color alone) В·
retention/erasure.

### Lens 7 вЂ” policy / legal
min-rest (EU WTD 11h) В· weekly cap (FLSA OT >40h as pay-trigger; EU 48h avg) В·
minor youth-labor (school-hours, nightly caps) В· mandatory meal/rest breaks
(coverage hole = relief assignment) В· fair-workweek advance-notice +
predictability/reporting-time pay + clopening В· minimum-staffing (e.g.
nurse-ratio) В· CBA/union (seniority pick-order, distinct from law) В·
protected-leave (FMLA/parental/jury/medical) В· disability modified-duty
accommodation В· external-commitment accommodation (religious / court-order,
hard non-negotiable) В· multi-jurisdiction rule selection (per location/person) В·
works-council co-determination approval В· disparate-impact / discrimination
audit of auto-assignment В· joint-employer / concurrent-employment aggregate
hours В· co-presence / two-person / lone-worker relational coverage В·
schedule-as-external-contract SLA (franchisor/client/insurer) В· statutory
record-keeping min-retention.

## Blind spots вЂ” needs human/field input

The critic's load-bearing output. These are gaps that **exist** but cannot be
closed by a generator/critic вЂ” the missing fact is real business/domain reality
that must come from a named human or field source. Naming the gap is first-class
output; inventing the missing value would be worse.

- **Actual jurisdiction(s)** вЂ” every legal threshold (min-rest hours, weekly cap,
  minor caps, clopening gap, predictability-pay schedule, holiday-pay rules,
  works-council applicability) is jurisdiction-specific. в†’ *legal counsel per
  deployment site.*
- **Real legal retention windows** вЂ” GDPR/CCPA erasure maximum vs labor-law
  minimum-keep are opposing statutory numbers. в†’ *legal / records-management.*
- **Weekly labor budget figure + pay-rate model** вЂ” to make the Budget ceiling a
  concrete hard/soft constraint. в†’ *finance / ops.*
- **Whether a CBA/union exists and its clauses** вЂ” seniority guarantees, premium
  triggers, max-consecutive-days are contract text, not inferable. в†’ *HR / labor
  relations.*
- **Solver objective weights** вЂ” the true business priority between labor-cost,
  preference-fit, and fairness. в†’ *product / ops decision.*
- **Acceptable generation latency + scale ceiling** вЂ” the real distribution of
  employees Г— shifts Г— locations. в†’ *field measurement, not a guessed SLA.*
- **Demand-forecast methodology** вЂ” how `required_headcount` is actually derived
  (sales model / footfall / manual). в†’ *field interview with operations.*
- **Fairness metric + acceptable disparity threshold** вЂ” Gini vs variance, and
  the legal/ethical tolerance for protected-class disparity in auto-assignment.
  в†’ *legal + ethics review.*
- **Notice-of-record event** вЂ” which timestamp (publish / delivered / acknowledged
  / first-view) legally starts the predictive-scheduling clock. в†’ *counsel,
  jurisdiction- and case-law-dependent.*
- **Multi-site authority model** вЂ” for a shared employee, whether a "home"
  schedule has veto or first-come-wins. в†’ *org policy.*
- **Override taxonomy** вЂ” which minimums are hard-illegal (never overridable) vs
  soft-policy (overridable with justification / dual sign-off). в†’ *management +
  legal sign-off.*
- **ACA measurement method** вЂ” look-back vs monthly, and whether benefits-threshold
  crossings hard-block or only advise. в†’ *HR policy.*
- **Joint-employer aggregate-hours handling** вЂ” declare-and-attest vs conservative
  cap vs explicit out-of-scope for concurrent/agency employment. в†’ *legal +
  product scope decision.*
- **External-contract minimums** вЂ” franchisor/client/insurer staffing covenants
  are in contracts the engine has no feed for. в†’ *contract review.*
- **Minor school-calendar source** вЂ” per-district session/holiday calendars have
  no canonical feed. в†’ *external integration / data-source decision.*
