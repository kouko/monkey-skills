# Canon: Visual communication / graphic design

Agent-facing recall insurance, consulted at the propose-candidates step as a
completeness audit — never shown raw to the user.

Including but not limited to the entries below; doctrine content stays in
the model (this list carries name + fits-when hint + stability note +
source only).

Grounded in `docs/loom/research/2026-07-10-principles-canon-base-lists.md` §3.

**Axis A — cultural/graphic movements only.** This file covers movements,
identity philosophy, and typographic/color systems. UI surface treatments
(flat / skeuomorphic / neumorphic / glassmorphic and their era-boundaries)
are the **surface-treatment axis**, which is **not decided at this station**:
it is decided downstream at the **DESIGN station** (the `loom-interface-design`
plugin's `design-system` skill), which owns that canon list. Do not propose
surface treatments from here.

**Downstream of the tone & manner anchor.** The primary visual anchor is the
product's 3-5 **tone & manner** adjectives, derived from its values / Product
Principles. Axis A sits **downstream of the tone & manner anchor**: it supplies
**mood / creative-direction inspiration** for candidates the adjectives have
already constrained. It is **never a pick-one menu** — the agent does not ask
the user to choose a movement *as* the visual direction, and a movement is
never a selection criterion in its own right.

**Anti-costume law.** A movement may enrich candidates but **never overrides a
PRINCIPLES value**. Its formal vocabulary is inspiration; the values are
non-negotiable — when the two collide, the movement loses. Worked example: a
low-stimulus constitution still excludes Memphis, however apt the mood.

**Popularity head**: Swiss Style, Bauhaus, Material Design — crowd out
e.g. Memphis, Kapferer's Prism,
Kenya Hara/MUJI, Japanese postwar tradition, and the whole art/subculture and
non-Western band above (Pop Art, Pixel Art, Vaporwave, Ukiyo-e, Kawaii,
Superflat, Socialist Realism, Dunhuang, 月份牌, HK neon, Guochao …). If a
draft's candidate set is only these, re-check the full list above —
cultural entries included — before proposing.
