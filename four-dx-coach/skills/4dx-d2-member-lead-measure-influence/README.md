# 4DX D2 — Member Lead Measure Influence

**English** | [日本語](README.ja.md) | [繁體中文](README.zh-TW.md)

> Team-member scope — map your personal sphere of influence on the team's lead measures (which you didn't choose), pick 1-2 focus leads, surface no-influence honestly.

## When this skill activates

- "Our team's lead measures are set — how do I influence them?"
- "I'm on a 4DX team, leads are picked, what do I actually do?"
- "Which lead measure is *mine* to move?"
- "I support all of them but feel stretched thin."
- 「team の lead measure は決まったが、自分はどう関われる？」
- 「團隊的 lead measure 已經訂了，我怎麼影響？」

## What it does (the protocol in brief)

Socratic influence-mapping — agent does NOT invent the map; member surfaces honest answers per lead.

1. **Comprehension gate** — list the team leads verbatim; halt to D1-member-comprehension if unknown
2. **Ladder check** — one-sentence causal chain per lead from lead → Team WIG (gap = leader homework)
3. **Per-lead influence audit (0-5)** — "from where you sit, this week, no permission required, can you move it?"
4. **Map check** — interpret distribution: multiple-high / specialist / all-low → routes to step 5 or step 6
5. **Focus pick** — name 1-2 primary-focus leads; cap is hard (4+ recreates whirlwind)
6. **No-influence escalation** — if all leads <3, surface to leader honestly: "I don't see a daily-influence handle"
7. **Daily question anchor** — "most important thing today to advance [focus lead]?" → D4 commitment input
8. **Output influence card** — leads / scores / focus / what-I'll-do / open questions for leader

## When NOT to use

| Situation | Where to go instead |
|---|---|
| Solo personal-WIG context (you pick your own leads) | `4dx-d2-personal-lead-measure-discovery` |
| Leader picking / facilitating leads | `4dx-d2-team-lead-measure-facilitation` |
| Don't yet know team WIG / lead measures | `4dx-d1-member-team-wig-comprehension` |
| Prepping a weekly commitment | `4dx-d4-member-commitment-prep` |
| Debriefing a missed commitment | `4dx-d4-member-account-debrief` |
| Disagreeing with the leads themselves (litigation) | 1:1 with leader; not coaching |
