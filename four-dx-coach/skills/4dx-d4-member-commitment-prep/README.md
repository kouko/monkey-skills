# 4DX D4 — Member Commitment Prep (Walk-In Ready)

**English** | [日本語](README.ja.md) | [繁體中文](README.zh-TW.md)

> Coach you (the team member) to prepare a SPECIFIC, lead-measure-moving commitment BEFORE you walk into your team's WIG Session — sharpened against five filters, in 5-10 minutes.

## When this skill activates

- "Help me prepare my commitment for next WIG Session"
- "What should I commit to at this week's WIG Session?"
- Last commitment was vague or didn't get kept; you suspect the commitment itself was the problem
- You don't want to look bad in front of peers/leader and want a private rehearsal
- You keep committing to whirlwind tasks that feel urgent but don't move the lead

## What it does (the protocol in brief)

Private prep, personal-coach voice TO you. Agent is allowed to push back on bad commitments — never to dictate:

1. **Gate — confirm lead measure is known (≤1 min)** — if you can't name it → halt, route to comprehension skill
2. **Look back at last week's commitment (~1-2 min)** — kept / partial / not kept; one root cause if not
3. **Generate candidate commitments (~2-3 min)** — canonical question: "ONE most important thing to move the team's lead measure, within your own control"
4. **Apply the five filters (~3-4 min, the core)** — Specific / Lead-measure-moving / Within own control / Self-chosen / Scope-cap (1-2 max)
5. **Rehearse the wording (~1 min)** — say it verbatim; correct any backsliding to vague language
6. **Hand off to the session (≤1 min)** — walk in, don't soften under social pressure

## When NOT to use

| Situation | Where to go instead |
|---|---|
| Solo user with no team WIG Session | `4dx-d4-personal-wig-session` |
| AFTER the session — reflecting on last week | `4dx-d4-member-account-debrief` |
| You don't know the team's lead measure | `4dx-d1-member-team-wig-comprehension` first |
| Leader facilitating the meeting | `4dx-d4-team-wig-session-lead` |
| Daily standup / sprint task / OKR check-in commitment | Different artifact — don't conflate |
