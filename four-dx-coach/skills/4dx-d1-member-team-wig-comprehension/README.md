# 4DX D1 — Member Team-WIG Comprehension

**English** | [日本語](README.ja.md) | [繁體中文](README.zh-TW.md)

> Coach you (the team member) to understand the team WIG you've been handed — what it says, why it was chosen, what the lead measures are, and what your personal slice is.

## When this skill activates

- "I joined a team running 4DX; how do I understand my role in their WIG?"
- "My manager set a team WIG but I don't know what I'm supposed to do."
- You can't recite the team WIG verbatim in *From X to Y by When* form
- You know the WIG but not the strategic rationale (compliance without commitment)
- You know the WIG and lead measures at team level but can't locate YOUR slice

## What it does (the protocol in brief)

Socratic dialogue. The agent does NOT invent answers — surfaces gaps and sends you to ask:

1. **State the team WIG verbatim** — measurable X, Y, deadline. If you can only paraphrase → halt and assign homework: ask your leader
2. **Surface the strategic rationale** — why this WIG, what alternative was declined? If shrug → ask your leader
3. **Surface the team's lead measures** — predictive + influenceable weekly behaviors, NOT the WIG restated
4. **Locate your personal slice** — recurring repeatable behavior you control that contributes to a team lead measure
5. **Translate into a daily/weekly commitment** — "Every [day/week] I will [verb + measurable count]"
6. **Install the daily question** — *"What is the most important thing I can do today to advance my team's lead measure?"*
7. **Output the comprehension card** — gaps marked honestly as "ask my leader" if needed

## When NOT to use

| Situation | Where to go instead |
|---|---|
| Picking your own solo WIG (no team context) | `4dx-d1-personal-wig-defining` |
| You ARE the leader choosing the team WIG | `4dx-d1-team-primary-wig-selection` |
| Ongoing weekly cadence | `4dx-d4-personal-wig-session` (solo) or session-lead/member-prep |
| Team has a "goal" but no scoreboard / lead measures / cadence | Flag mismatch — they have a goal, not a WIG |
| Fundamentally disagreeing with the team WIG | 1:1 with leader, not coaching |
