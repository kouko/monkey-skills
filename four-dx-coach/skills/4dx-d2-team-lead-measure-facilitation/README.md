# 4DX D2 — Team Lead Measure Facilitation

**English** | [日本語](README.ja.md) | [繁體中文](README.zh-TW.md)

> Team-leader scope — facilitate the team to discover 2-3 lead measures for an already-defined Team WIG. Veto-don't-dictate.

## When this skill activates

- "How do I run a lead-measure session with my team without just telling them the answer?"
- "My team brainstormed 12 candidate leads — how do I help them narrow to 2-3?"
- "Should I prepare draft lead measures before the team meeting?"
- 「team で lead measure を一緒に決めたい。どう facilitate すればいい？」
- 「帶 team 一起找 lead measure，但我不想直接給答案，怎麼引導？」

## What it does (the protocol in brief)

Socratic facilitation — leader holds the WIG anchor and the two-axis test; team authors the leads.

1. **Team WIG gate** — leader states Team WIG in *From X to Y by When*; halt to D1 cascade if vague
2. **Anti-dictate inoculation** — brief leader on 4 facilitation rules (no pre-write / veto-not-dictate / silence is the tool / cap at 2-3)
3. **Wide brainstorm (5-10 candidates)** — team authors; leader pushes for counter-intuitive options
4. **Two-axis test as a team** — predictive (1-5) AND influenceable (1-5), with one-sentence causal chain per lead
5. **Drop ≤3 on either axis** — leader names WHY (vetoes) but does NOT propose replacement
6. **Narrow to 2-3, prefer one frequency + one quality pair** — team picks; leader vetoes >3
7. **Operational definition** — team writes; leader proofreads for vagueness
8. **Causal-chain commitment** — team writes one paragraph: "If we hit leads weekly for 6 weeks, lag moves by X because…"
9. **Output** — 2-3 team-authored leads + handoff to D3 / D4
