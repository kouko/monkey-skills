Fillable template ported verbatim from the book. Copy this worksheet,
fill in the cells, then validate the result against

## 5-step instruction (verbatim)

1. Brainstorm ideas for the WIG.
2. Brainstorm lag measures for each idea (From X to Y by When).
3. Rank in order of importance to the organization or to the overall
   WIG.
4. Test your ideas against the checklist on the following page.
5. Write your final WIG(s).
