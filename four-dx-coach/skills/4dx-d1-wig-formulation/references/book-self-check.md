# D1 WIG-quality self-check — "Did You Get It Right?"

> Source: McChesney/Covey/Huling/Thele/Walker (2021), *The 4 Disciplines of
> Execution* 2nd ed., Simon & Schuster, p 220.

The official FranklinCovey verification gate for a freshly-formulated Team
WIG. Run after the WIG Builder (`../worksheets/wig-builder.md`, book p 219)
has produced a candidate WIG. All seven items should clear before the WIG
is committed.

## Verbatim checklist (Team-WIG scope, as written in the book)

- [ ] 1. Have you gathered rich input both top-down and bottom-up?
- [ ] 2. Will the Team WIG have a clear, predictable impact on the overall
  organizational WIG or strategy, not just on team performance?
- [ ] 3. Is the Team WIG the most impactful thing the team can do to drive
  achievement of the overall WIG?
- [ ] 4. Does the team clearly have the power to achieve the WIG without
  heavy dependence on other teams?
- [ ] 5. Does the WIG require the focus of the entire team, not just of
  the leader or a subgroup?
- [ ] 6. Is the lag measure written in the format "From X to Y by When"?
- [ ] 7. Can the WIG be simplified any further? Does it start with a
  simple verb and end with a clear lag measure?

## Personal-scope lens (adaptation for solo / personal WIG)

For personal-scope use within this multi-file scope-flex skill, read each
item with "Team WIG" → "WIG" and "team" → "you". The book's verbatim
phrasing above remains canonical; this lens is an adaptation note, not a
rewrite.

- [ ] 1. Have you gathered rich input both top-down (mentors, role
  models, mission) and bottom-up (your own daily friction, what's
  actually within your control)?
- [ ] 2. Will the WIG have a clear, predictable impact on your larger
  life / career / project mission, not just on isolated activity?
- [ ] 3. Is the WIG the most impactful thing you can do to drive
  achievement of that larger mission?
- [ ] 4. Do you clearly have the power to achieve the WIG without heavy
  dependence on others?
- [ ] 5. Does the WIG require focus across your week, not just an
  occasional side-project moment?
- [ ] 6. Is the lag measure written in the format "From X to Y by When"?
- [ ] 7. Can the WIG be simplified any further? Does it start with a
  simple verb and end with a clear lag measure?

## Verdict

- All 7 PASS → WIG clears the book's official D1 self-check; proceed to
  D2 (lead measures).
- Any FAIL → return to `../worksheets/wig-builder.md` step 4 (test
  ideas against the checklist) and revise before commit.
