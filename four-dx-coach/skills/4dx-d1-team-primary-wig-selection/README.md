# 4DX D1 — Team Primary WIG Selection

**English** | [日本語](README.ja.md) | [繁體中文](README.zh-TW.md)

> Coach you (the leader) to pick the team's Primary WIG by brainstorming candidate Battles and filtering on a 2x2 — Importance × Feasibility — converging to ONE WIG (cap ≤2 Primary, ≤3 team WIGs).

## When this skill activates

- "Help me pick our team's Primary WIG" / "where should our team focus this year"
- "We have too many strategic goals" — leader can't converge 5+ candidates
- The current Primary WIG feels too broad ("total revenue") and is producing no traction
- Debating Approach A (one WIG) vs Approach B (twin WIGs across segments)
- Leadership team comfortable ranking *importance* but skipping *feasibility*

## What it does (the protocol in brief)

A consultant-to-leader dialogue. The agent facilitates the leadership team's pick — does NOT pick for them:

1. **Frame mission + strategic context** — and confirm cascade-vs-selection (cascade redirects out)
2. **Brainstorm 5-10 candidate Battles** — go broad, no pre-filtering
3. **Score each on Importance (1-5)** — would breakthrough transform the team?
4. **Score each on Feasibility (1-5)** — can the team move it via behavior change it controls?
5. **Plot the 2x2 (text grid)** — visual placement
6. **Filter to upper-right** — pick the ripple-effect leader (Covenant Transport pattern)
7. **Approach A vs B decision** — single Primary WIG (default) vs twin across genuinely separate segments
8. **Four-trap checklist** — too many / too broad / unmeasurable / mission-disconnected
9. **Express in *From X to Y by When* form** — pass the stranger test
10. **Output WIG + parked Battles list** — declare Approach explicitly

## When NOT to use

| Situation | Where to go instead |
|---|---|
| Solo / individual goal | `4dx-d1-personal-wig-defining` |
| Org WIG already set; need team's slice | `4dx-d1-team-wig-cascade` |
| Team member trying to understand a chosen WIG | `4dx-d1-member-team-wig-comprehension` |
| Pre-strategy team with no defined mission | Define mission first |
| Reactive / on-call domain (whirlwind IS strategic) | `4dx-meta-team-strategy-triage` |
